Deployment Workflow

Three simple actions to get your personal brand online.

01

Fork & Configure

Fork the repository to your account. Enable GitHub Actions and set the Source to gh-pages in Settings.

02

Personalize Content

Edit website/siteConfig.js to update your name, tagline, social links, and project details.

03

Auto-Deploy

Push your changes. The Docker container builds the site via Actions and publishes it globally.
